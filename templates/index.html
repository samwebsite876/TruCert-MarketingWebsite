The Problem

01

As it currently stands, employers rely on traditional methods such as “word of mouth” or printed documents to verify academic qualifications in Jamaica.

02

This outdated approach leaves room for fraudulent activity, as individuals may present fake or altered degrees.

03

Without a secure verification system, organizations risk hiring unqualified candidates, leading to reputational damage and legal concerns.

Our Solution

TruCert is a blockchain-based system that allows universities to issue secure digital certificates. These certificates are tamper-proof and can be verified instantly from anywhere.

How It Works

1. Register

Universities register and get approved.

2. Issue

Degrees are stored securely on blockchain.

3. Receive

Students receive their digital degrees.

4. Verify

Employers verify instantly using students credentials.
