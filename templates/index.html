About TruCert

What is TrueCert?

TrueCert is a digital certificate verification platform that allows institutions to issue secure digital certificates and allows employers to verify them quickly. The system uses blockchain technology to help protect certificate records from being altered or forged.

Secure Digital Certificates

Institutions can issue digital certificates that are easier to store, share, and verify.

Blockchain-Based Verification

Certificate proof is recorded using blockchain technology to support tamper-resistant verification.

Fast Employer Checks

Employers can quickly confirm if a certificate is authentic without relying on slow manual checks.

What is Blockchain Technology?

Blockchain is a digital record system that stores information in a way that is difficult to change. Once information is added, it becomes part of a secure record that can be checked later.

In TrueCert, blockchain is used to store proof that a certificate was issued and has not been altered. This helps employers verify academic credentials faster and with more confidence.

The Problem

Traditional Verification Is No Longer Enough

Traditional certificate verification is often slow, manual, and unreliable. Employers may have to contact institutions directly or depend on printed documents, which can be altered, duplicated, or forged.

Degree fraud creates risk for employers, institutions, and graduates.

Fake Certificates

Printed certificates can be forged or edited, making it difficult to confirm whether a qualification is real.

Slow Verification

Manual checks can delay hiring and decision-making because employers often have to wait for institutional responses.

Lack of Trust

Employers need a reliable way to confirm that qualifications are authentic and have not been altered.

How It Works

How It Works

TrueCert follows a secure step-by-step process to issue, store, and verify academic certificates in a faster and more reliable way.

01

Institution Issues a Certificate

An institution creates and issues a digital certificate through the TrueCert platform.

02

Unique Hash Is Generated

The system generates a unique cryptographic hash for the certificate.

03

Proof Is Stored on the Blockchain

The certificate proof is securely recorded on the blockchain to support authenticity.

04

Student Receives or Claims Certificate

The student can then receive or claim the issued digital certificate.

05

Employer Enters or Scans Details

An employer enters the certificate details or scans the certificate to begin verification.

06

TrueCert Confirms Validity

The platform checks the certificate record and confirms whether it is authentic and valid.
